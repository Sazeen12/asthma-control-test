Asthma Control Test (ACT)

1. During the past 4 weeks, how often did your asthma symptoms (e.g., wheezing) bother you?

2. During the past 4 weeks, how often did you use your rescue inhaler?

3. During the past 4 weeks, how much did asthma keep you from getting as much done at work, school, or home?

4. During the past 4 weeks, how controlled would you say your asthma has been?

5. In the past 4 weeks, how often did your asthma cause shortness of breath?
